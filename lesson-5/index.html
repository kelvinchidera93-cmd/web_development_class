Apple

Nature's perfect snack, our fresh apples are handpicked for their vibrancy and crispness. Rich in fiber and antioxidants, apples are not just delicious but incredibly beneficial for your health. Whether you prefer sweet red apples or tart green varieties, Fruitella brings you the finest selection from local orchards.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in soluble fiber that aids digestion
  • Contains quercetin, an antioxidant that supports brain health
  • Promotes heart health and lowers cholesterol
  • Boosts immune system with vitamin C
  • Naturally low in calories yet filling

Mango

Known as the "King of Fruits," mangoes are a tropical delicacy packed with nutrition and natural sweetness. Our premium mangoes are sourced at peak ripeness, offering a burst of flavor and a creamy texture that melts in your mouth. Perfect as a standalone snack or incorporated into your favorite dishes.

Health Benefits

  • Exceptional source of vitamin C and vitamin A
  • Contains natural enzymes that aid digestion
  • Rich in antioxidants that fight inflammation
  • Supports immune system function
  • May improve skin health with vitamin E

Lemon

These bright, zesty lemons are an essential kitchen staple. Fresh and juicy, they are perfect for cooking, beverages, or adding a refreshing touch to your meals. Our lemons are sourced directly from trusted growers, ensuring maximum freshness and flavor. Use them to enhance your dishes with natural acidity and brightness.

Health Benefits

  • Excellent source of vitamin C for immunity
  • Aids natural detoxification and digestion
  • Boosts metabolism and aids weight management
  • Supports hydration when added to water
  • Contains compounds that improve heart health

Pineapple

Tropical, sweet, and incredibly nutritious, our pineapples bring sunshine to your table. Each fruit is selected for its golden color and aromatic sweetness. Rich in the digestive enzyme bromelain and loaded with vitamin C, pineapples are both a delicious treat and a nutritional powerhouse. Enjoy them fresh, grilled, or blended.

Health Benefits

  • Contains bromelain enzyme that aids digestion
  • Packed with vitamin C for immune support
  • Natural anti-inflammatory properties
  • Promotes bone health with manganese
  • May aid post-workout recovery and muscle repair

Oranges

Whether you prefer eating them fresh, squeezing them for juice, or using them in recipes, our oranges deliver consistent quality and taste that you can rely on.

Health Benefits

  • Packed with vitamin C for strong immunity
  • High in dietary fiber for digestive health

  • Aids collagen formation for healthy skin
  • Provides natural energy boost without sugar crash
